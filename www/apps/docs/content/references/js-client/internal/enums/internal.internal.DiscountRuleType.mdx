# DiscountRuleType

The possible types of discount rules.

## Enumeration Members

### FIXED

Discounts that reduce the price by a fixed amount.

___

### FREE\_SHIPPING

Discounts that sets the shipping price to `0`.

___

### PERCENTAGE

Discounts that reduce the price by a percentage reduction.
